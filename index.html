About Us

Building Financial Strength Since 2005

Biashara Tosha Sacco, formerly Gastameco Sacco Ltd, was established to help members access reliable and affordable financial services. The Sacco operates through BOSA (Back Office Services Activity) and FOSA (Front Office Service Activity).

To mobilize savings and provide affordable and diversified products and services to our members in order to uplift their living standards.

To be a Sacco of excellence in our area of operation.

Membership

Growing Stronger Together

The Sacco membership has grown steadily over the years, reaching 4,433 members in 2019. Based on projections, the Sacco is expected to maintain a modest growth rate of 10% in 2020 and 5% annually thereafter.

This growth momentum is projected to increase membership to 6,223 members by 2025, with an estimated 85% likelihood of members having fully paid their share capital — strengthening the Sacco’s financial base and sustainability.
